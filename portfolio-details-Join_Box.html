Join the boxes Did you learn physics in school? Then try your hand at this awesome game! A little gravity, a little rubbing and you're done! Sounds easy, doesn't it? Well, let's see you. Why do you have to join these 2 boxes? The first reason is "just for fun", and it's completely true. Why not? Everyone deserves to relax after the a hectic pace of a work day, and this game knows how to help with that! Simple actions, Ð° fresh and cool design, a wide variety of tasks - this game has hundreds of levels so you can have lots of fun in your spare time. The second reason is for strategy. The application has a difficult puzzle in which you have to think carefully to overcome the level: find the best way to join the 2 boxes. Watch out for the center of gravity, which often misleads you.
